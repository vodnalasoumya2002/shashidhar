15-year-old Dennis Mulberry has been the lead singer of high-school band Crystal City Killers for 3 years now. After the band's bold decision to start making their very first EP, he meets Harvey Huxley-the perfect candidate for their band's designer. As the bond between Dennis and Harvey blossoms more and more each day, Dennis realises that his journey of accepting who he is may come earlier than originally planned. A heartwarming LGBT comic about being transgender in the world of love.
